## ğŸ® How to Play

The Simon Game challenges players to remember an increasingly long sequence of colors.

1.  **Start:** Press any key on the keyboard to start the game.
2.  **Sequence:** Simon will flash a color and play a corresponding sound.
3.  **Repeat:** You must click the button with the same color.
4.  **Advance:** Simon will add another color to the sequence, making it longer.
5.  **Challenge:** Continue repeating the longer sequence until you make a mistake.
6.  **Game Over:** If you click the wrong color, the game ends, and your final score (level) is displayed. Press any key to restart and try to beat your high score!
